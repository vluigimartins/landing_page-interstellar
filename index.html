Synopsis

Interstellar is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan, released in 2014. The film is set in the near future, where Earth is facing a global environmental crisis.

The story follows a group of astronauts on an interstellar mission to find a new home for humanity, as the planet Earth is no longer habitable. With a combination of scientific theories and stunning visual effects, the film explores themes of love, time, and survival.
